About

I throw ceramics in Atlanta, working to create pots with dynamic forms and practical function.

I strive to create pieces that are timeless, functional, and memorable. Each piece is made slowly and deliberately, and practically.

The ceramics I make are meant to be used and lived with. I'm interested in how objects can become part of daily life - even if just on a shefl.

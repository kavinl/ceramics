About

I'm a ceramic artist based in Atlanta, working to create forms that challenge form and function.

My practice centers on creating pieces that are timeless, functional, and memorable. Each piece is made slowly and deliberately, and practically.

The ceramics I make are meant to be used and lived with. I'm interested in how objects can become part of daily life - even if just on a shefl.
